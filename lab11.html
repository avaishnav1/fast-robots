Note for Future Students

Since I spent way too long debugging this lab, here's a piece of advice: if you are 100% confident that both your Arduino and your Python code are correct AND you have triple checked with the teaching team-- try changing your ToF sensor OR borrowing a friend's robot... Also ensure that you're in long-distance mode for your sensor... I hope all y'all don't suffer as much as I did to find this fix :)) Good luck!!
